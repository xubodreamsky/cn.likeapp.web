Our Story

We wanted something better. We wanted a text messaging app that was beautiful, easy to use, fun, and most importantly, safe. So we built it. Most of our team had never even met in person, but we came together on the nights and weekends and made something that we love. It took a few months, and there were challenges, but there’s no greater feeling than shipping a product like Like. For us, the ultimate payoff is not money or fame. It’s having consumers fall in love with our product, knowing that it enhances their lives. We strive to build a better product everyday and we’re just getting started. Thank you for using Like. We would love to hear your feedback.
